Easy Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a lasagna recipe found on thecozycook.com. It inclues instructions on cooking the lasagna, reheating it, and storing it properly.

Additionally, there are links to related recipes like Baked Ravioli.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 ox. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Ttalian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomate paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
    • <1i>teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add diced onions.
  3. Add ground beef and sausage and increase to medium-high heat.
  4. Add chicken broth, marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water and cook noodles to al dente.
  7. Lay the cooled noodles flat on wax or parchment paper.
  8. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch dish.
  9. Add 4 lasagna noodles.
  10. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by 1 and 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  11. Finish with 4 more noodes, the rest of the ricotta mixture and the sauce.
  12. Top with Mozarella cheese and bake for 35 minutes.
